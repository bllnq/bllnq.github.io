Driver Assistant

Technology

 

We have developed a driver assistant technology that uses a robust heurstics-based algorthim to detect the aspect and context of traffic signal, sign, lane departure and forward collision.

Traffic Light Detection

The key innovation is within the robustness and hierarchy of the heuristics and their implementation with a computationally efficient framework.

Our goal is to refine and optimize the heurstics-based algorhim and code; develop an app version for use with existing hardware on mobile phones; design and construct a prototype dash-mounted camera-based sensor that incorporates the heurstics code in order to field test and demonstrate the performance and accurany of the technology.
